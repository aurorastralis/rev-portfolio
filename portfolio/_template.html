The Brief

To support the launch of a new flagship chocolate bar, I was brought on to craft a socially native campaign that would cut through the sugar-saturated noise of Instagram and TikTok. The brief was simple: “Please buy this chocolate, because it’s awesome.”

I turned that into a bold, self-aware creative hook — mixing joyfully over-the-top language with ultra-shareable formats, trending audio, and visual humor. Think: snackable scripts, cheeky CTA lines (“Tap to unwrap”) and captions designed to charm both chocoholics and algorithm gods.

From playful short-form video scripts to headline-style post copy, everything was geared toward maximum scroll-stopping appeal — and making the audience feel like they’d discovered their new favorite indulgence.
